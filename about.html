About Us

This website is a space where high school students share their perspectives on different music genres.

It’s entirely from a teen viewpoint, so you get to see what students think about pop, rock, hip-hop, classical, and more.

Each genre is explored through reviews, personal opinions, and experiences, giving a fresh, relatable insight into music.

The site lets students compare tastes, discover new songs, and understand why certain genres resonate with their peers.

It’s less about technical music theory and more about how music feels and connects with high schoolers.
